Delete Account and Data

App: Fishing for Hadith

Developer contact: fishingforhadith@gmail.com

How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your Fishing for Hadith account and associated app data, email us at fishingforhadith@gmail.com using the email address linked to your Google sign-in account.

Please include the subject line: Delete Fishing for Hadith account.

Data that will be deleted

  • Player profile information stored for the app, such as display name, email, and user ID.
  • Game progress, including score, coins, bait, fish collection, achievements, and selected topics.
  • Leaderboard entry connected to the same user account, where applicable.

Data that may be kept

We may keep limited records only when required for security, legal, abuse prevention, or troubleshooting purposes. These records are not used for gameplay.

Processing time

Account deletion requests are normally processed within 30 days after we verify that the request came from the account owner.

If you only used the app without signing in, you can clear local app data from your Android device settings. Local-only guest data may not be linked to an online account.
