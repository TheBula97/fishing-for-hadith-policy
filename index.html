Privacy Policy for Fishing for Hadith

Effective date: April 29, 2026

Fishing for Hadith is an educational Android app developed by Hasbullah Hamzah. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how users can contact us.

Information We Collect

The app may collect the following information when users choose to sign in with Google:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Firebase user ID
  • Profile sign-in status

The app may also store gameplay progress, including:

  • Score and best score
  • Coins and bait count
  • Fish collection and achievements
  • Selected game mode and topics
  • Leaderboard entries

How We Use Information

We use this information to provide app functionality, including player profiles, cloud progress sync, achievements, and leaderboard features.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Firebase services provided by Google, including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. These services help provide Google sign-in and cloud data storage.

Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Data is only processed through services needed for app functionality, such as Firebase.

Local Use Without Email

Users may use the app with a local player name without signing in. In that case, profile and progress information may remain stored locally on the user's device.

Data Security

Data sent to Firebase is encrypted in transit. Firestore security rules are used so signed-in users can access their own progress data.

Data Deletion Requests

Users may request deletion of their cloud profile and progress data by contacting us at fishingforhadith@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for educational revision. It is not submitted as a Google Play Families app. If the app is used by younger students, supervision by a parent, guardian, or teacher is recommended.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at fishingforhadith@gmail.com.
